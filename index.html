About me

Thank you for visiting my site! My name is Virginia. I am a full stack Web Developer. I love writing code that results in creative and functional applications that would help people.


I studied Psychology and worked as a Pharmacy Technician. As a pharmacy technician I learned how to solve different types of issues in a methodical way which has helped me with many challenges that come up when building an application. I look forward to working with a team of developers and adding my unique skills to the team.
